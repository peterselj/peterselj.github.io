Why I write.

I don't keep a blog anymore (I did for about 10 years). Instead I write essays into Google Docs, where it's easier for my readers and I to comment (in-line) and converse (via Gmail) than any other web platform I could think of.

I don't chase clicks. My metric of choice is not "scale," or millions of viewers, or thousands of shares. My metric is dialogue. I write to explore challenging ideas and provoke intimate conversations.

In 2016, every 1,000 words I wrote turned into over 3,000 words in comments from 20 or fewer readers. That's my metric, and I'm proud of it.
